About Me

What’s up, Seattle! I’m Theo, a tattoo artist from Europe. I now call Washington State my home, but I work all across the USA.

I'm a professional artist and bring both my ideas and yours to life on skin. 

Specializing in dark and horror themes, I've developed my own style over the years that can handle even the most complex requests from my clients. I've previously been a resident artist in studios in Russia, Germany, Denmark, and NYC, and now I'm excited to be here in Seattle!

HOW TO CREATE YOUR OWN UNIQUE DESIGN

01

DESCRIBE YOUR IDEA IN DETAIL

Tell me about your idea and where you want the tattoo. Mention any characters, objects, or scenes you want to include in the tattoo.

02

SEND REFERENCES YOU LIKE

These can be photos of similar tattoos, sketches, or artworks. This will help me understand the style you prefer.

03

SCHEDULE A PERSONAL CONSULTATION

How much does it cost? It's free! This helps us get better results and communicate more effectively. We’ll understand each other better by discussing the design in prison.

04

CREATE THE DESIGN WITH ME

We will have a one-hour consultation, during which we will lay the foundation for your unique project.

We will measure the placement area and create your design on a graphic tablet, considering your anatomy.
